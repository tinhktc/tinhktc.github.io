Privacy Policy

Sleep Sounds – White Noise & Calm Timer
Last updated: 27 Dec 2025

1. Introduction

Sleep Sounds – White Noise & Calm Timer (“the app”, “we”, “our”) is a simple utility application designed to help users relax, focus, and improve sleep quality through sound playback and timer features.

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled when you use the app. The app does not require user registration, login, or personal identification and is designed to operate without collecting personally identifiable information.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, the app and its integrated services may automatically process limited non-personal information necessary for proper operation and monetization:

2.1 Usage & Analytics Data

Collected to improve app quality and stability:

  • App open events
  • Feature usage (play, pause, stop, timer actions)
  • Device type, OS version, app version
  • Crash reports and error diagnostics

This data may be collected through Firebase Analytics and related diagnostic services.

2.2 Advertising Data

The app displays advertisements using Google AdMob to support free usage. AdMob may automatically process:

  • Advertising ID
  • Basic device information
  • Ad interaction data

The app requests non-personalized ads by default and does not knowingly enable personalized advertising for sensitive categories.

3. How Information Is Used

All collected data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Ensuring proper app functionality
  • Improving performance and stability
  • Delivering and measuring advertisements
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share user information for any other purpose.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services which operate under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics

Google’s privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

Non-personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above and in accordance with the retention policies of the third-party services used.

6. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect all data handled by the app.

No sensitive personal data, login credentials, health data, or payment information is stored by the app.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will promptly take appropriate steps to delete such information.

8. User Control & Choices

You may stop all data processing at any time by uninstalling the app.

You may also control your device’s advertising settings to limit ad tracking.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.

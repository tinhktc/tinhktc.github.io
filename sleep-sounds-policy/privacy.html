Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Sleep Sounds collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Sleep Sounds collects minimal data necessary for app functionality:

  • App Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics through Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app and improve user experience.
  • Crash Reports: We collect crash logs through Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix bugs, ensuring app stability.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (device model, OS version) for compatibility and optimization purposes.
  • Local Preferences: Your app settings, favorites, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and prevent crashes
  • Analyze app usage patterns to enhance features
  • Ensure app compatibility across different devices

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can learn more about AdMob's privacy practices at Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics: For app analytics and usage statistics. Data is collected anonymously.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting and error tracking to improve app stability.

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your personal preferences, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not store personal information on our servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, anonymized data with service providers who assist us in operating the app, subject to confidentiality agreements.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (though this may affect app functionality)
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
